Specialization

Quantitative Finance

Using statistical and quantitative methods to analyze and predict the markets, and apply programming tools to produce robust investment strategies. Work revolves around creating mathematical models that are used to assess and manage financial systems, potential risk, and timing of trades.

Operations Research

The application of similar ideas to larger, more complex decisions that concern the operations of systems, such as businesses and networks of machines. Making these decisions using management science practices entails employing mathematical methods in order to solve a numerical version of the problem at hand.

Data Analytics

Collecting, organzing, and converting raw data into actionable insights. It includes a range of tools, technologies, and processes used to find trends and solve problems by using data. Data analytics can shape business processes, improve decision-making, and foster business growth.

Data Visualization & Modeling

Data Visualization involves presenting data visually using graphics. Data Modeling visualizes the entire or only some parts of an information system to establish the relationship between data points and structures. It shows the relationship between various entities in a database.
